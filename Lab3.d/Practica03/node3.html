Subversion and teams

Your instructor may decide to group students in teams for this project. If that is the case, it is also advisable to have Subversion support the effort of the team. This means setting a subversion repository somewhere in a server machine where members of the team belong to a user group and users of that group shall have all privileges to act on t eh repository. This is a technical matter that is not the main subject matter of the course and will not be discussed further. We only insist that using version control in a team is truly useful and full documentation on svn can be found in
http://svnbook.red-bean.com/
